Auto-Rename Your Images with AI

Vision Language Agent "looks" at your images, understands their content, and renames them intelligently. Best of all, it runs 100% locally on your machine—your data never goes to the cloud.

What Can It Do?

An autonomous assistant powered by local Large Vision Models (LVM).

🧠

Smart Analysis

Uses the Llama 3.2 Vision model to analyze image content with incredible accuracy, generating concise names like "black-motorcycle.jpg".

🔒

100% Local & Private

Powered by Ollama. Your files and images are never uploaded to the internet; they stay completely secure on your device.

⚡

Autonomous Lifecycle

Automatically starts Ollama in the background, downloads the required AI model by itself, and cleans up VRAM when finished.

🛡️

Collision Protection

If two files get the same name, a smart numbering system steps in to prevent your existing files from being overwritten.

How It Works?

1

Select Folder

2

Sit Back and Relax
